MALAVALLI

Malavalli is a town in Mandya district.It has famous places like Gaganachukki falls,Narasimha temple,Muthatti forest. People there talk prominently in Kannada and are have their unique slang, one can easily recognise them because of that.Mostly they grow Paddy,Ragi, Sugarcane.They love their Ragi mudde-Naati koli saaru.They celebrate a festival called "SIDI HABBA" which is celebrated for 3 days in the month of January Or February.
